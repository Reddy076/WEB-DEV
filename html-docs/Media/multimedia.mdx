# Multimedia

Multimedia comes in many different formats. It can be almost anything you can hear or see, like images, music, sound, videos, records, films, animations, and more.

## HTML Video
The `<video>` element specifies video, such as a movie clip or other video streams.

```html
<video width="320" height="240" controls>
  <source src="movie.mp4" type="video/mp4">
  <source src="movie.ogg" type="video/ogg">
  Your browser does not support the video tag.
</video>
```

### Video Attributes
- `controls`: Adds play, pause, and volume controls.
- `autoplay`: Starts playing automatically (often blocked by browsers if not muted).
- `muted`: Mutes the audio.
- `loop`: Restarts the video when finished.
- `poster`: An image to show while the video downloads.

## HTML Audio
The `<audio>` element specifies audio, like music or other audio streams.

```html
<audio controls>
  <source src="horse.ogg" type="audio/ogg">
  <source src="horse.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
  Your browser does not support the audio element.
</audio>
```
